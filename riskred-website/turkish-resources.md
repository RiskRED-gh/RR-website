## Risk RED Resources in Turkish

### Basic Disaster Awareness — ABCD - Temel Afet Bilinci Eğitim Programı

**Description:** Handbook. 1 sheet Family Safety Plan. Training workshop materials. Videos in Turkish for family & household safety and resilience.

**Purpose:** Basic public earthquake risk awareness education.

- [koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/abcd.asp](http://www.koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/abcd.asp)
- [ipkb.gov.tr — Güvenli Yaşam Videoları](https://www.ipkb.gov.tr/e-kutuphane-kategorisi/egitim-ve-bilinclendirme/guvenli-yasam-videolari/) *(based upon work originally created by Risk RED affiliates)*

### Structural Awareness for Seismic Safety — Depreme Karşı Yapısal Bilinç

**Description:** Handbook. Training workshop materials. Videos in Turkish for non-technical public education on construction for seismic safety.

**Purpose:** To create consumer demand and knowledge for policy-compliant construction. More in-depth training available for technical and vocational training students.

- [koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/dkyb.asp](http://www.koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/dkyb.asp)
- [afet.zeytinburnu.istanbul](https://afet.zeytinburnu.istanbul/egitim/depreme-karsi-yapisal-bilinc-egitimi/)
- [afet.kadikoy.bel.tr](https://afet.kadikoy.bel.tr/egitimler/depreme-karsi-yapisal-bilinc-egitimi-dkyb)
- [ipkb.gov.tr — training modules](https://www.ipkb.gov.tr/e-kutuphane-kategorisi/egitim-ve-bilinclendirme/egitim-modulleri-ve-materyalleri/) *(based upon work originally created by Risk RED affiliates)*

### Non-Structural Mitigation — Yapısal Olmayan Tehlikelerin Azaltılması

**Description:** Handbook. 1-day training workshop materials. Video game. Videos in Turkish for non-technical public education on non-structural mitigation for seismic safety.

**Purpose:** To create consumer demand and knowledge for policy-compliant construction. More in-depth training available for technical and vocational training students.

- [koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/yota.asp](http://www.koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/yota.asp)
- [ipkb.gov.tr](https://www.ipkb.gov.tr/e-kutuphane/depreme-karsi-yapisal-olmayan-risklerin-azaltilmasi_50/)
- [ipkb.gov.tr PDF](https://www.ipkb.gov.tr/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/Depreme-Karsi-Yapisal-Olmayan-Risklerin-Azaltilmasi-3.pdf) *(based upon work originally created by Risk RED affiliates)*

### School Disaster Management — Okullarda Afet Yönetimi

**Description:** Handbook. 1–2 day training workshop materials on school safety management.

- [tosya.meb.gov.tr PDF](https://tosya.meb.gov.tr/meb_iys_dosyalar/2019_06/26120652_okullarda_afet_ve_acil_durum_yonetimi_el_kitabi.pdf)
- [facebook.com/okulafetveacildurumyonetimi](https://www.facebook.com/okulafetveacildurumyonetimi/)

Save the Children/Risk RED have also created an easily adaptable toolset in other countries:

1. School Safety Self-Assessment Digital Toolset with automated school and geographically aggregated reports.
2. Education Sector Post-Disaster Damage and Needs Assessment Digital Toolset with automated school and just-in-time digital dashboard.

### Community Disaster Volunteers — Toplum Afet Gönüllüleri

**Description:** Handbook. 2-day training workshop materials for community safety volunteers.

- [koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/tag.asp](http://www.koeri.boun.edu.tr/aheb/tag.asp)
