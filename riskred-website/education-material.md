## School Safety and Educational Material

Risk RED develops educational materials with a focus on adults (the general public), children, and schools. Some specific work to which Risk RED has contributed:

### Activities for adults and children related to disaster preparedness

- ["Go-Bag" Scavenger Hunt](/files/gobag-scavenger-hunt.pdf) for children aged 6–12, and ["Go-Bag" International Display](/files/gobag-international-display.pdf) for all ages.
- IFC. 2010. *Disaster and Emergency Preparedness: Activity Guide for K to 6th Grade Teachers*. IFC (International Finance Corporation), World Bank Group, Washington, D.C. [Full text](/files/ifc-activity-guide-k-6.pdf).
- IFC. 2010. *Disaster and Emergency Preparedness: Guidance for Schools*. IFC (International Finance Corporation), World Bank Group, Washington, D.C. [Full text](/files/ifc-guidance-for-schools.pdf).
- ShakeOut: Public earthquake safety exercise. [2008 Southern California Full Report](/files/shakeout-2008-full-report.pdf) and [2008 Southern California Summary](http://www.riskred.org/schools/shakeout2008article.pdf).

### Tackling disaster myths

Promoting [The Need for an Evidence-Basis for Earthquake Survival Tips](/files/evidence-basis-earthquake-survival-tips.pdf).
