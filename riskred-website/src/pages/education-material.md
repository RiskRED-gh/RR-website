## School Safety and Educational Material

Risk RED develops educational materials with a focus on adults (the general public), children, and schools. Some specific work to which Risk RED has contributed:

### Activities for adults and children related to disaster preparedness

- ["Go-Bag" Scavenger Hunt](https://www.riskred.org/_files/ugd/96ab52_7a5a32f03b04451ab7899db8947cd0ba.pdf) for children aged 6–12, and ["Go-Bag" International Display](https://www.riskred.org/_files/ugd/96ab52_76ed110047c6499c816c35859fa3be33.pdf) for all ages.
- IFC. 2010. *Disaster and Emergency Preparedness: Activity Guide for K to 6th Grade Teachers*. IFC (International Finance Corporation), World Bank Group, Washington, D.C. [Full text](https://www.riskred.org/_files/ugd/96ab52_66e4c9b321e5488cb71cb49befd64ba9.pdf).
- IFC. 2010. *Disaster and Emergency Preparedness: Guidance for Schools*. IFC (International Finance Corporation), World Bank Group, Washington, D.C. [Full text](https://www.riskred.org/_files/ugd/96ab52_718d336732084d0c8e786525edcd04dd.pdf).
- ShakeOut: Public earthquake safety exercise. [2008 Southern California Full Report](https://www.riskred.org/_files/ugd/96ab52_1d582b76a8f14c20a4bbbf16c562e7cf.pdf) and [2008 Southern California Summary](http://www.riskred.org/schools/shakeout2008article.pdf).

### Tackling disaster myths

Promoting [The Need for an Evidence-Basis for Earthquake Survival Tips](https://www.riskred.org/_files/ugd/96ab52_53328e7d03134f22bc4227a95b8e885d.pdf).
